Our Story

Fire Is Our Language

Across Asia, the wok is more than a pan — it is tradition, rhythm, and instinct. Wok Kitchen was created to honor this ancient craft while presenting it in a modern dining experience.

Modern Asian Kitchen

We combine classic wok techniques with contemporary flavors. Every dish is cooked over open flame, fast and bold, preserving the soul of street cooking.

Dehiwala Roots

Located at 17/1 Hill Street, Dehiwala, Wok Kitchen brings premium Asian street food to a refined, modern space.
